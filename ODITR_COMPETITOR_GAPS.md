# Øditr - Competitor Gaps & Exploitation

## Semrush
- **Strength**: Marketing, keyword research, backlinks.
- **Weakness**: Lacks deep code-level debugging, real-time technical inspection, developer-first workflows, CI/CD regression checks, exact implementation fixes, and JS hydration debugging.
- **Øditr Action**: Do not compete on backlinks/keywords. Win on exact developer-friendly fixes and CI/CD integration.

## Screaming Frog
- **Strength**: Deep crawling and technical SEO.
- **Weakness**: Complex UI, lacks historical monitoring, cloud dashboard, alerts, business impact scoring, and SaaS-style reporting.
- **Øditr Action**: Provide a simpler, more visual, and actionable UI. Offer live cloud dashboard and historical monitoring.

## DebugBear
- **Strength**: Core Web Vitals, performance monitoring, RUM.
- **Weakness**: Narrow scope.
- **Øditr Action**: Combine performance with Technical SEO, Accessibility, Security, AI-agent readiness, and developer fix snippets.

## Datadog / New Relic / FullStory
- **Strength**: Observability, session replay.
- **Weakness**: Massive enterprise scope, heavy setup.
- **Øditr Action**: Don't become a full observability tool. Borrow the concept of connecting technical issues to business/user impact and showing post-deployment changes.
